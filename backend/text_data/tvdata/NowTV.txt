## FAQ

### What devices can I use to watch NOW TV?

Watch NOW TV through the Xfinity Stream app and enjoy on up to three devices at the same time.

**Desktop:** macOS, Windows PC

**Mobile:** Amazon Fire tablets, Android phones and tablets, iPhone & iPad

**TV:** Amazon Fire TV, Roku, Xfinity Flex, Xumo Stream Box, Xumo TV, Chromecast, AirPlay

### What do I get with NOW TV?

With NOW TV, you get access to 125+ NOW TV channels and Peacock Premium (currently $7.99/mo value).

NOW TV features live and On Demand content. To watch, download the Xfinity Stream app.

With Peacock, you get hit movies, exclusive Originals, live sports, and so much more. To watch Peacock Premium, activate your Peacock subscription and download the Peacock app.

### How does the Xfinity Stream app work?

The Xfinity Stream app lets you access NOW TV and all the entertainment you love — at home and on the go. Watch Xfinity Stream on Xumo Stream Box, or install it on a compatible device. [Learn more](https://www.xfinity.com/get-stream) or download it now for [Apple](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/xfinity-stream/id731629156) or [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.xfinity.cloudtvr&pli=1).
