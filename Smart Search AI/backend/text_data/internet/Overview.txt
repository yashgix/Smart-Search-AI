Mbps? Gig? A quick tutorial on internet speed, please.

Internet speed measures how long it takes data to get from the Internet to your computer, in megabits per second (Mbps). We offer speeds from everyday-browsing 50 Mbps to industry-leading 10-gigs (10,000 Mbps). Take our quiz to [find the speed you need.](https://speedtest.xfinity.com/)
